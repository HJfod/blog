# So Long, and Thanks for All the Mods!

> This post is currently just a draft, it's not yet finished :)

Hello!

If I had to throw a guess, then you're probably here from Twitter or Discord or wherever you saw my announcement image. To confirm the news right off the bat, yes: **BetterEdit has officially been sunset**. This applies to all of my other mods like GDShare and Backups as well, as **I am retiring from actively making GD mods**. I still have a managerial role on Geode, and have other plans too I'm going to go over a little later, but as for actively writing new C++ code for mods, yup, I'm done with that.

So in this post, I just want to make a quick dive into what's going on, what I plan on doing from now on, and finish it off with a little historical recap of the GD modding scene as I've got to witness it. Strap in!

## What is happening to your mods?

My mods (including BetterEdit, GDShare, Backups, Trashcan, and Quick Volume Controls) are all, from this day forward, **abandonware**. This means that while they may still work, they will eventually stop doing that. Maybe a Geode update causes them to receive a game-crashing bug, maybe another popular mod becomes incompatible with them, or maybe a new GD update is released and they're just never updated to support the new version. Whatever it is, I am not going to fix those mods anymore. Once they die, they're dead.

To be very clear, **this is not a split-second decision, this is not an angry rant, this is not me quitting the community entirely with hate and bitterness in my heart**. I've just grown out of modding, and very much satisfied with what I've managed to get done in the years I've been doing this. It's time I get some new hobbies in my life.

**If any young modder out there wants to take over updating my mods, feel free to do so!** Although, a bit of warning: you'd probably rather make your own versions instead. Maintaining an old C++ codebase is incredibly dull, and especially moreso for one you did not even write yourself. You'll have a lot more fun if you make your own Backups mod that is compatible with my old version, rather than just simply porting mine over to new GD updates.
